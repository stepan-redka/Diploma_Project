Dietary Options
Gluten-free pasta available for all pasta dishes (+$3). Vegan cheese option for pizzas. Full allergen menu available upon request. Our kitchen handles nuts, dairy, and shellfish.

Private Events
The Tuscany Room accommodates up to 40 guests for private events. Minimum spend of $1,500 required on weekends. Custom menus available with 2 weeks advance notice. Contact events@bellaitalia.com for inquiries.

Loyalty Program
Earn 1 point per dollar spent. 100 points = $10 reward. Birthday month bonus: double points on all purchases. Sign up at the restaurant or through our mobile app.
